Name

unzip

Usage

builtin-function

Syntax

(unzip seq)

Description

Returns a list of the elements at even positions and at odd positions of seq.

user > (unzip (series 10))
it: ((1 3 5 7 9) (2 4 6 8 10))
user > (unzip (range 10))
it: ((0 2 4 6 8) (1 3 5 7 9))
