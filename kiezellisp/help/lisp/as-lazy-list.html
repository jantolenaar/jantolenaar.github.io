Name

as-lazy-list

Usage

builtin-function

Syntax

(as-lazy-list seq)

Description

Converts a sequence to a lazy list.

make-html > (let z (as-lazy-list (series 5)))
it: z
make-html > (first z)
it: 1
make-html > (rest z)
it: (2 3 4 5)
make-html > z
it: (1 2 3 4 5)
make-html > (last z)
it: (5)
make-html > z
it: (1 2 3 4 5)
